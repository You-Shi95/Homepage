Biography

You Shi has been with the College of Electrical Engineering and Control Science, Nanjing Tech University (NJTech), China, as a Lecturer, since December 2024. He obtained Ph.D. degree from the College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), China, in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Bing Chen and Prof. Changyan Yi.

His research interests include human-centric digital twin, stochastic optimization, online optimization and learning for service and resource management in mobile edge computing, Industrial IoT and UAV systems.
